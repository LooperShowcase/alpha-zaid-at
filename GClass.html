History: The G-Class was first introduced in 1979 and has undergone several updates and redesigns while maintaining its distinctive appearance and rugged capabilities. It has served various roles, including military and civilian use, and continues to be a flagship model in Mercedes-Benz's SUV lineup. Current Models: As of recent years, the G-Class is available in multiple configurations, offering a blend of luxury, performance, and off-road capability to cater to different customer preferences.
